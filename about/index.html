I’m Hanna Shibata, an independent Multidisciplinary Designer who is looking forward to being your next collaborator. I would love to contribute my extensive design expertise honed through years of experience working in-house and at agency firms.

After starting my career as a package designer in Tokyo, I expanded my role into branding and communication as an in-house creative, where I managed relations with internal and external stakeholders, and built a creative team from scratch.

In 2018, I left Tokyo to pursue my graduate studies at Pratt Institute Pratt Institute and continue my design career in the United States.

I’m now taking time to expand my skill set and explore the digital realm of design.
